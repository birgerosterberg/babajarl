Cosmic Earth Vibrations

The living energy of the universe
Resounds within the range of our senses
Capturing vibrations, nature, music
The inner world of sound

Gaze into the heavens on a star-studded night
Surrender to the vastness of space
Synchronized vibrations
The heart of human consciousness

Constantly in motion, our universe vibrates.
Moon’s rotation exactly synced
Orbits around the earth
The same face in all phases

“Om,” the vibration of the universe
Frequency of energy
Connecting all things
An ocean of love surrounds us.

Composing positive energy
Through kindness and compassion
Serving one another
High frequencies established.
